Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FlowMa collects, uses, and stores information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (optional): If you sign in with Apple, we may receive your Apple user identifier, and may receive your email and name based on your Apple authorization settings.
  • User content: Text you enter in FlowMa and audio you choose to upload for speech-to-text features.
  • App usage data (local): Basic event logs may be stored locally on your device for app behavior analysis and debugging.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core features such as task planning, AI-generated content, and weekly insights.
  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • Improve app quality and reliability.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication tokens are stored in iOS Keychain on your device.
  • Some app state may be stored in UserDefaults on your device.
  • Data sent to our server is transmitted over HTTPS.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with service providers as needed to operate FlowMa features.

5. Your Choices

  • You can use guest mode in supported cases.
  • You can request account deletion inside the app (when signed in).

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at 851068226@qq.com.
